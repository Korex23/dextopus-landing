Two products. One integration.

Deposit brings money into your product from your users. Withdrawal sends it out to wherever they need it. Both run on the same two endpoints. Your infrastructure stays exactly as it is.

01 · Deposit
↓

Your users fund their accounts from anywhere

A user sends Bitcoin, USDT on Tron, WBTC on Ethereum, SOL on Solana, USDC on Celo, GUN on Gunz, or any supported token. Dextopus detects the transaction, converts it, and delivers your chosen stablecoin to your recipient address. Your user never touches a bridge. You receive exactly what you configured.

Two address types available. Static addresses are permanent, one per user per network, reusable forever. Flexible addresses are generated per session, single use.

02 · Withdrawal
↗

Your users get their funds out, on the chain they use

A user wants to withdraw to Tron, Bitcoin, USDC on Katana, RON on Ronin, ETH on zkSync, or a chain your product has never integrated. You generate an address, send the funds to it, Dextopus converts and delivers to the user's external wallet. Your product now reaches destinations it never could before.

Settlement infrastructure. With an operator experience nobody built before.

Every business that integrates Dextopus gets permanent user addresses, a full operator dashboard, real-time webhook notifications, and on-chain fee collection. Out of the box.

First ⬛

Static deposit addresses

One permanent address per user per network. Generate once, active forever. One EVM address covers every EVM chain. One Tron address. One Solana address. One Bitcoin address. Your users save it like a bank account number.

First ▦

Operator dashboard

See every address you generated, every transaction, every deposit that landed or failed. Full visibility into your integration, no internal tooling required.

⚡

Webhook notifications

Real-time HTTP POST when deposits are created, completed, or fail. Cryptographically signed. Automatic retries with exponential backoff. Your backend knows the moment money moves.

◈

On-chain fee collection

Set your fee in basis points. Add your wallet once. Every deposit and withdrawal pays you directly on-chain, automatically. No invoicing. No settlement delays.

↺

Automatic refunds

Send a supported token to the wrong chain? It converts. Send a chain's native token we don't support? It refunds automatically to your specified refund address. Your users are protected.

∞

Non-custodial throughout

Dextopus never holds funds. Every transaction executes through audited smart contracts, on-chain and traceable end to end. No new custody relationship introduced.
